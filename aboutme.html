About Me

As a budding UX/UI designer and researcher, I am on an exciting journey of discovery and innovation. Though I am new to the field, my eagerness to hit the ground running sets me apart. With a background in education, I bring a unique perspective, understanding deeply how people learn and interact with information. My education at Columbia University Bootcamp has equipped me with the foundational skills needed for UX/UI design and research. I have learned to empathize with users, understanding their needs and pain points, and I am proficient in using design tools and methodologies to create intuitive and engaging user experiences. My approach is research-driven, ensuring that my designs are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and user-centered. I value feedback and iteration, constantly seeking ways to improve my work. My experience as a teacher has honed my ability to communicate complex ideas clearly and effectively, a skill that is invaluable in collaborating with stakeholders and team members. In short, I am a dedicated and passionate new UX/UI designer and researcher, ready to leverage my unique background and skills to create meaningful and impactful user experiences. My enthusiasm and commitment to continuous learning will undoubtedly propel me forward in this dynamic field.
