American Clothing Pickup Case Study

American Clothing Pickup Case Study

Donate Your Way

Problem Statement

American Clothing Pick Up aims to provide an efficient donation process, but users struggle with unclear instructions, inefficient navigation, and accessibility issues. Which makes the donation process more tedious than it should be.

Possible Solution

As a UX/UI researcher and designer, we prioritize simplifying the American Clothing Pick Up website to enhance user experience, focusing on clear instructions, intuitive navigation, and improved accessibility for a seamless donation process.

Target Audience

Donors

Recipients

Our Role

Design Strategy,Empathy Mapping,Prototyping,Visual Design,Problem Solution,User Persona,Usability Testing,Wireframes,User Research,Competitive Analysis,Competitive Analysis,User Flow,Storyboard,

Design Thinking Process

First step, was conduction review on the website to see what component was usable and what new ideas we can incorporate to make the user experience more enjoyable and unforgettable. Then we proceed to the interview process which gave us a scope on the user experience which allows to fulfilled their needs.

Project Timeline

The project spanned over three months, with each phase carefully planned to meet deadlines and ensure quality outcomes.

Empathize Phase

Interview Questions

We conducted interviews with 15 donors and 10 recipients. Key questions included:

  • What was easy or difficult about the navigation?
  • Were there any points where they felt lost or confused?
  • How intuitive did they find the menu and layout?
  • Any suggestions for improvement?

Key Insights Derived

We learned how frequent people make donation to organizations

What motivates people to make a donation

If they priorities knowing where they're donation is being used

Which method they prefer to use when donating

Quantitative Research

These pie chart are results from our survey we conducted to get insight on people motivation to give back, donation history, the importance of tracking where their donation is being used for, which method they use when donating

Define Phase

We developed user personas to represent our target users, ensuring that our design solutions were user-centered and addressed real pain points.

User Flow

The user flow diagram helped us visualize the steps users take from scheduling a donation to completing the pickup, ensuring a smooth and logical process.

Task Analysis

Task analysis allowed us to break down the donation process into smaller tasks, identifying areas for improvement and streamlining the user experience.

Interview Notes

Our interview notes revealed common themes such as the need for better communication and a more intuitive interface. These insights guided our design decisions.

High-Fidelity Prototype

The high-fidelity prototype incorporated all the design improvements and was tested with users to validate its effectiveness.
